**[SCREEN: Animated trichome stages]**

## Production Guidelines

### For Video Creators

#### Equipment Needed
- **Camera**: 1080p minimum, 4K preferred
- **Audio**: Lavalier or shotgun microphone
- **Lighting**: Soft, even lighting
- **Screen Recording**: High quality, clear cursor
- **Plant Samples**: Multiple examples for demos

#### Best Practices
- **Keep it concise**: 5-10 minutes max per topic
- **Show, don't tell**: Demonstrate everything
- **Clear audio**: Ensure narration is clear
- **Pace yourself**: Don't rush explanations
- **Visual aids**: Use callouts, highlights, zooms
- **Real examples**: Use actual plant problems
- **Call to action**: Subscribe, like, check docs

#### Engagement Techniques
- **Hook in first 5 seconds**: "In this video, you'll learn..."
- **Use patterns**: "First... Second... Finally..."
- **Highlight key points**: Visual emphasis
- **Preview results**: Show outcome early
- **Clear CTAs**: "Subscribe for weekly tutorials"
- **End screens**: Related videos, subscribe

#### Accessibility
- **Closed captions**: Add to all videos
- **High contrast**: Ensure text is readable
- **Clear fonts**: Use sans-serif, adequate size
- **Audio descriptions**: For visual-only content
- **Downloadable resources**: PDFs, checklists
